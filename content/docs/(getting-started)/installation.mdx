### Setting Up Vynk

Now, here's where the magic happens and your kind of easy life starts -

1. First, initialize Vynk in your project (if you haven't already). Choose your weapon of choice:

<InstallationCommand command={'init'} />

2. This will magically create a vynk.config.json file in the root of your project.
3. You can configure this file according to your taste and the cli will work accordingly
