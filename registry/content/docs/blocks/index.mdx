Ah, the moment you've been waiting for - let's talk about these magnificent building blocks!

Ever tried to build a landing page that doesn't look like it was designed in the 90s? Or a feature section that actually makes users want to read? We've got you covered. And let's talk about animations. Not the kind that make your users' eyes bleed, but the smooth, subtle ones that make your app feel alive. Our animated blocks are like a good cup of coffee - they give your app that extra kick without the jitters.

## Features

- **Pre-built Layouts**: Ready-to-use combinations of components that look great out of the box
- **Customizable**: Modify blocks to match your brand with ease
- **Dark Mode**: Built-in dark mode support for all blocks
- **Responsive**: Blocks that look great on all screen sizes
- **TypeScript**: Full TypeScript support for better developer experience
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions and animations that don't compromise performance
